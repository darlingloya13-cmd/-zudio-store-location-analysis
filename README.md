# -zudio-store-location-analysis
Data analysis and feasibility scoring to identify potential new Zudio store locations across India.

📊 Dataset store Overview: Zudio Stores Across India This dataset provides a comprehensive mapping and demographic analysis of 967 Zudio retail stores across India to evaluate market reach and expansion potential.

🔑 Key Features Included: Store Identity & Location:

Store Name: Name of the individual Zudio store outlet.

State, City, & Address: Complete geographical location details for every store nationwide.

Economic & Demographic Indicators:

Estimated Population: Total population count for the corresponding city.

Per Capita Income: Average income level per person in that region.

Malls: Number of shopping malls present in the city.

Target Audience & Feasibility Metrics:

Buyers Count: Target demographic count (estimated customer population aged 15 to 50 years in that city).

Potential Location: A calculated score evaluating the suitability and feasibility of opening/sustaining a Zudio store in that region.

📊Dataset filtered1 Contains data specifically for cities where NO Zudio store is currently present, used to identify prime potential expansion locations. The mall includes Number of shopping hubs/major commercial retail stores (such as Vishal, V-Mart, V2, etc.).

📊Dataset result with already store contains cities and states that already have at least one Zudio store but possess high market demand to support additional store openings. Includes locations with a Priority Index > 0.5 indicating high density, strong buyer potential, and room for further market penetration.

📊Dataset result with no store contains cities and states that do not currently have any Zudio store, but present a prime expansion opportunity with a Priority Index > 0.5.
